Inspiration Behind the AW10 Collection.

This season, GIA explores contrasts — softness against structure, edges that define stillness. Each piece is designed to feel deliberate, wearable, and quietly defiant.

The collection draws from urban geometry and muted palettes, inspired by architecture, sculpture, and the strength of simplicity. Every silhouette reflects an intersection of craft and clarity.
